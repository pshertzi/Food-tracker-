Our Purpose

We are a brand that cares about helping each other build healthy habits around food. Today it can be hard to choose what to cook for dinner and know if you are making a healthy choice. We are here to offer you a place to find recipes with their nutrition facts, log meals with nutrition facts, and locate a store near you if you need an ingredient for your next favorite recipe.



Why Log?

By tracking what you eat for a given amount of time, you will get a big picture of your eating habits. The log can help you and your doctor understand how your eating habits affect your parts of your daily life such as weight, energy level, or gastro issues. Once you know this, you can make changes to your diet to improve your life.
